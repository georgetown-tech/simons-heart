        Companies and Simon's Heart<\/span><\/h3>\n

        Simon's Heart is run like a small business. Providing free heart screenings, promoting research, advocating for lifesaving legislation and raising awareness are all done with a focus on the organization's strategy and return on investment.<\/p>\n

        We have a few ways for your company to get involved. Let's talk about how our combined resources can protect hearts and save lives. Of course, you can always\u00a0make a donation<\/a>\u00a0or volunteer<\/a>.<\/p>\n

        Cause Marketing<\/span><\/h3>\n

        Imagine a campaign that could authentically tell our stories \u2013 a campaign that raised awareness of your brand and our lifesaving message. We have been able to craft such campaigns with Speedo<\/a> and the Philadelphia Flyers. What can we do together? Email us<\/a>.<\/p>\n

        Get an AED<\/span><\/h3>\n

        Is your company prepared for a cardiac event? An AED is a small investment with a huge return. It is also the only tool that can save someone in cardiac arrest. It belongs next to your fire extinguisher. Learn about our Small Business AED Program. Protect your employees. Support our mission. Download our information sheet<\/a>.<\/p>\n

        Partnerships<\/span><\/h3>\n

        Big change comes from big investments of time and capital. Simon's Heart is always working on a few big projects that require funding, and will bring us closer to making heart screenings a standard of care. To learn more about how you can make a big impact and create a legacy, contact Darren<\/a>.<\/p>\n

        Sponsorships<\/span><\/h3>\n

        Simon's Heart hosts two major events per year: Simon's Soiree and Simon Says Golf. We also host heart screenings and conduct awareness events like AED Madness and the Overtime Challenge. We sponsor a sports cardiology fellowship and operate a crowdfunding site called GotAED.\u00a0There is lots of great work going on and we\u2019d love for you to be a part of it. To learn more, contact us<\/a>.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div><\/div><\/div><\/div><\/div>","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
