Every coach in the world has a playbook. Every school in the world, is prepared for a fire. However, when it comes to sudden cardiac arrest, the #1 cause of death of student athletes, we are completely unprepared.<\/p>\n

What’s the first thing you do when a student collapses? Who makes the phone call? Does anyone know CPR? Is there an AED device? Who’s going to get it?<\/p>\n

It is best to figure out the answers to these questions before the\u00a0student\u00a0collapses in the hallway or on the field. That is the purpose of a cardiac emergency response plan. It’s your playbook and practice manual rolled into one.<\/p>\n
