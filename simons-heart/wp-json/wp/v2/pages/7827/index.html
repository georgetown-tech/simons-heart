Automated External Defibrillator (AED Device)<\/h2>\n

When you car battery dies, you need jumper cables. When your heart stops, you need an automated external defibrillator (AED). It's that simple. The AED device is a critical part of the cardiac chain of survival.<\/p>\n

It\u00a0is the only tool that can save someone in cardiac arrest. After determining that a student's heart isn't beating properly, the AED will deliver a shock and get the heart back into its normal rhythm.<\/p>\n

How much does an AED cost? They start around $1,300. They are easy to use because they\u00a0are automated - turn it on and a voice provides instruction. The device will also decide if the student needs a shock.\u00a0In other words, don't worry about electrocuting your friend.<\/p>\n

Simon's Heart promotes the availability of AEDs in schools, community centers and sports clubs through its crowdfunding website, GotAED<\/a>. It also donates AEDs through its awareness campaigns, the Sudden Death Shootout, Chain of Survival Relay and AED Madness. Click here<\/a> to see our AED recipients.<\/p>\n
