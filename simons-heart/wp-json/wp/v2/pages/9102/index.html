Bounce. Bounce. Bounce.<\/p>\n

The bounce of a ball sounds like the beat of a heart, right? In fact, when you walk, your heart beats slowly and the ball is probably bouncing slowly. If you run, the beat of your heart and dribble speed of the ball will increase. That\u2019s how it is supposed to work - it's normal.<\/p>\n

It may not be normal<\/span> if your heart beats fast and you\u2019re not moving fast (unless you just got scared or excited). There may be a problem<\/span> if it takes too long for your heart to slow down after you slow down. Bottom line \u2013 you need to KNOW your heart.<\/p>\n

Learn the warning signs<\/a> so that you can talk to your parents, coach and\/or trainer if something doesn\u2019t feel right.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div><\/div>
