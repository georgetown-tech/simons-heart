    Statement of Privacy<\/span><\/h3>\n

    The Simon's Heart foundation is committed to protecting your privacy. This Statement of Privacy applies to the Simon's Heart foundation website and governs data collection and usage at all the Simon's Heart foundation sites and services; it does not apply to other online or offline sites, products or services. Please read the Simon's Heart foundation Statement of Privacy. The Simon's Heart foundation is a general audience website, intended for users of all ages. Personal information of all users is collected, used and disclosed as described in this Statement of Privacy.<\/p>\n

    Collection of Your Personal Information<\/span><\/h3>\n

    As a charitable, non-profit website, the Simon's Heart foundation collects personal information, such as your email address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Information collected by the Simon's Heart foundation is used solely for the purpose of processing donation payments and billing for event activities. The Simon's Heart foundation does not collect any information about your computer hardware and software.<\/p>\n

    The Simon's Heart foundation encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from The Simon's Heart foundation so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. We are not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the Simon's Heart foundation website.<\/p>\n

    Use of Your Personal Information<\/span><\/h3>\n

    The Simon's Heart foundation and its operational service partners collect and use your personal information to process donations and payments for events as requested. It is possible that the Simon's Heart foundation may also use personal information to inform you of other products or services available from the Simon's Heart foundation and its affiliates. The Simon's Heart foundation may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.<\/p>\n

    The Simon's Heart foundation does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. The Simon's Heart foundation may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your personal information (email, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party.<\/p>\n

    We occasionally hire other companies to provide limited services on our behalf, such as handling the processing and delivery of mailings, providing customer support, processing transactions, or performing statistical analysis of our services. We will only provide those companies the personal information they need to deliver the service. They are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose.<\/p>\n

    The Simon's Heart foundation does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.<\/p>\n

    The Simon's Heart foundation may access and\/or disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on the Simon's Heart foundation or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of the Simon's Heart foundation, including its website; or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of the Simon's Heart foundation or the public.<\/p>\n

    Personal information collected on this site may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country in which the Simon's Heart foundation or its affiliates, subsidiaries or agents maintain facilities, and by using this site, you consent to any such transfer of information outside of your country. The Simon's Heart foundation abides by the safe harbor framework as set forth by the U.S. Department of Commerce regarding the collection, use, and retention of data from the European Union.<\/p>\n

    Control your Personal Information<\/span><\/h3>\n

    The Simon's Heart foundation offers its customers choices for the collection, use and sharing of personal information. You may go notify us of your preferences by emailing info@simonsheart.org<\/a>. Please be sure to provide complete account information so we can identify you in our records.<\/p>\n

    You may also stop the delivery of future promotional email from the Simon's Heart foundation by responding directly to any email you receive with a request to remove you from the mailing list.<\/p>\n

    Security of your Personal Information<\/span><\/h3>\n

    The Simon's Heart foundation is committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. For example, we store the personal information you provide on computer systems with limited access that are located in controlled facilities. When we transmit highly confidential information (such as a credit card number) over the Internet, we use a secure payment gateway so your information is protected.<\/p>\n

    Changes to this Statement<\/span><\/h3>\n

    The Simon's Heart foundation will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. If there are material changes to this Statement or in how the Simon's Heart foundation will use your personal information, the Simon's Heart foundation will prominently post such changes prior to implementing the change. The Simon's Heart foundation encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how the Simon's Heart foundation is protecting your information.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div><\/div><\/div><\/div><\/div>","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
