Free AEDs for Schools<\/h1>\n<\/div>\n<\/div><\/div>
\n
\n\t

It's one of the best events in all of sport: March Madness. Everyone becomes an expert in bracketology, and all eyes are on the Road to the Final Four.<\/p>\n

Did you know that\u00a0male Division I basketball players are at the greatest risk of sudden cardiac arrest? They have a one in three thousand chance of experiencing it.<\/p>\n

Every year, we highlight the stories of NCAA players and teams that have come face to face with cardiac arrest. We also engage in a little AED Madness and give away free AEDs to local\u00a0youth facilities.<\/p>\n

Partnering with our local NCAA schools \u2013 Temple, St. Joe's, Drexel, Penn and LaSalle - we promote awareness at a home game, and ask the fans to select the youth\u00a0facility that will receive the free AED device.<\/p>\n
