Overtime Challenge<\/h1>\n<\/div>\n<\/div><\/div>
\n
\n\t

An automated external defibrillator is the only tool that can save someone in cardiac arrest. Once the pads are applied to someone's chest, the device analyzes the heart and determines if a lifesaving shock is needed. All you need to do is turn it on and apply the pads.<\/p>\n

AEDs are so important that we keep launching campaigns to distribute them. This is the Overtime Challenge. Sudden death is a great way to decide a hockey game. It is a tragic way to lose a teammate. Every time the Philadelphia Flyers enter a sudden death period, we donate an AED to a local youth facility.<\/p>\n

Do you need an AED where your kids learn or play? Send us<\/a> an email to be added to our donation list or start a campaign on our crowdfunding site, GotAED.<\/a> Are you a college hoops fan? Then you'll be interested in AED Madness<\/a>!<\/p>\n
