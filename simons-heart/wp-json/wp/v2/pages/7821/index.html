Sudden death<\/strong> is a great way to decide a game; not lose a teammate. We believe this is an important message for student athletes and sports fans. Every year, to kick off Simon Says Golf, participants surround the putting green for the Sudden Death Shootout. Using hockey sticks provided by the Philadelphia Flyers, everyone putts a numbered ball toward the hole. The ball closest to the hole wins tickets to a Flyers game and donates an AED to their youth facility of choice.\u00a0 It is a fun way to protect hearts and save lives.<\/p>\n
