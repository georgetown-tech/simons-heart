There are over eight million<\/a> student-athletes in the United States, which is defined as a student (middle and high school) engaged in interscholastic sports. When community, intramural and private school sports are considered, the number is much bigger.<\/p>\n

Athletics comes with risk. Students experience a wide variety of injuries. However, students should not die playing sports particularly from a heart condition that is detectable and treatable.<\/p>\n

Sudden cardiac arrest is the #1 (medical) cause of death of student-athletes. It takes the lives of thousands of children every day. There are a few reasons for this outcome. The student-athlete physical exam process is inconsistent and inadequate<\/a>. Coaches, parents and students are not educated<\/a> about the warning signs and conditions that lead to sudden cardiac arrest and death.
