HeartBytes is a national youth cardiac registry of seemingly healthy kids. It was developed and funded by Simon\u2019s Heart to fill the void of cardiac data on children. The data and images are crowdsourced by heart screening organizations (Deborah Hospital Foundation <\/a>and Safe Beat<\/a>) and made available to qualified researchers\u00a0(free of charge).<\/p>\n

The registry has received IRB approval from Jefferson University Hospital. Download the\u00a0Data Dictionary<\/a> to review our data subset. Digital ECG recordings are also available.<\/p>\n
