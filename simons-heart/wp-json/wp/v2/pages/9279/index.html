Simon's Heart was started for a boy named Simon who died before his time. Every chapter that we've written contains the names of families and children who have been impacted by heart conditions and\/or sudden cardiac arrest.<\/p>\n

Vision<\/h3>\n

A future in which parents and communities don\u2019t lose children to detectable and treatable heart conditions.<\/p>\n

Mission<\/h3>\n

To create and advance innovative and evidence-based programs, policies, and research that prevent sudden cardiac arrest and death in children, teens, and young adults.<\/p>\n

Values<\/h3>\n

Curiosity . . . about what causes, and what can prevent, sudden cardiac arrest and death in youth<\/p>\n

Integrity . . . in the evidence and data that drive our programs and policies<\/p>\n

Empathy . . . for\u00a0families, educators, coaches, pediatricians and communities impacted by sudden\u00a0cardiac\u00a0arrest<\/p>\n

Innovation . . . in programs that engage families and advance the science.<\/p>\n
