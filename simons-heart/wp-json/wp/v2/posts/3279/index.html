One of my favorite songs is Seasons of Love from RENT. \u00a0In it, the characters describe all of the ways to measure a year:<\/p>\n

In daylights, in sunsets, in midnights<\/em>
\nIn cups of coffee<\/em>
\nIn inches, in miles, in laughter, in strife.<\/em><\/p>\n

In truths that she learned,<\/em>
\nOr in times that he cried.<\/em>
\nIn bridges he burned,<\/em>
\nOr the way that she died.<\/em><\/p>\n

In five hundred twenty-five thousand six hundred minutes.<\/em><\/p>\n

As we look back at 2011 and toward 2012, we’re going to change the lyrics a bit.<\/p>\n

In heartbeats, in school kids, in volunteers
\nIn screenings (and cups of coffee)
\nIn generous donors, in ECHOs, in ECGs<\/p>\n

In truths that she learned (through raising awareness),
\nOr in times that he cried (because of a life-changing diagnosis).
\nIn corrective procedures performed,
\nOr stories of the thousands that died.<\/p>\n

In three thousand seven hundred and nine hearts checked, and thirty five lives changed.<\/p>\n
