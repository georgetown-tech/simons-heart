A\u00a0Federal Government Advisory Committee <\/a>is urging the use of this test.\u00a0 Two Governors (Maryland<\/a> and New Jersey<\/a>) recently signed laws mandating the use of this test.\u00a0 Clearly, the hearts of newborns are front of mind.<\/p>\n

These laws require that a Pulse Ox<\/a> test be performed on all newborns.\u00a0 The test measures the level of oxygen in the blood.\u00a0 Low levels can indicate problems with the heart and\/or lungs.<\/p>\n

Why is this important?\u00a0 The test is cheap and non-invasive.\u00a0 It leads to the detection of congenital (inherited) heart defects \u2013 the most common birth defect.\u00a0 The reality is that the Pulse Ox has been widely used for many years and is a standard of care in many major hospitals.\u00a0 However, these laws guarantee its use in ALL hospitals.<\/p>\n

Simon\u2019s Fund is very pleased with this development.\u00a0 The laws will save lives.\u00a0 They will raise awareness about the prevalence and prevention of heart conditions in children.\u00a0 They create a precedence of utilizing inexpensive and non-invasive methods to screen for heart conditions in children, which is core to Simon\u2019s Fund\u2019s mission.<\/p>\n

However, these laws don\u2019t replace our efforts.\u00a0 We are providing ECG exams to all newborns at the Corrine Santerian Newborn Clinic<\/a> because we believe this will detect other structural and electrical conditions that lead to sudden cardiac arrest and death.\u00a0 We are providing heart screenings for students around the Philadelphia area because far too many of them are dropping dead.<\/p>\n

Our screenings, which involve an ECG and Echocardiogram, do not look for the same conditions as the Pulse Ox.\u00a0 Furthermore, many of the conditions that are detected through our screenings may not be present at birth (the Pulse Ox is done 24 hours after birth).<\/p>\n

We need laws and initiatives like this.\u00a0 We need compassionate legislators and Governors.\u00a0 We need nonprofits to screen the hearts of students.\u00a0 There is plenty of room under this umbrella.\u00a0 There are plenty of ways to check hearts and save lives.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
