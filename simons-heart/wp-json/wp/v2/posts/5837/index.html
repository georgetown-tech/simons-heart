Today and always Simon, we think of you
\nWe think of your short life and the things your memory lives on to do<\/p>\n

It was just three short months that you were put on this earth
\nYour life was cut short just shortly after your birth<\/p>\n

Nine years may seem like such a long time
\nHowever, I know that your parents aching pain is for a lifetime<\/p>\n

Even though you are no longer physically here,you are saving lives
\nYou would be so proud of your Mommy and Daddy and all of their heart drives<\/p>\n

They have spread awareness across state after state
\nThey have helped saved people’s hearts who could no longer wait<\/p>\n

Every day we here about something that your Mommy and Daddy are making sure takes place
\nYour picture is on everything and we will always remember your beautiful, sweet \u00a0face<\/p>\n

Please know that Simon Sudman is a name that everyone does know
\nIt is you sweet boy that has made other lives glow<\/p>\n

I am thinking of you today, tomorrow and forever
\nYou have made your marks and will be forgotten, NEVER<\/p>\n

Darren and Phyllis,<\/p>\n

The pain that you have endured with the loss of Simon is immeasurable. I am fully aware that the loss of Simon is something you feel and stings at your heart each and everyday.<\/p>\n

Wherever you go, there are reminders that Simon isn’t here. I know you have to walk around seeing other children that you know would have been Simon’s best friends. I know there are dates and milestones that you know will not take place.<\/p>\n

Despite your unbearable pain, you are saving the world! You are selflessly devoting your life so that others do not have to experience this pain.<\/p>\n

I am sure there are still days where it’s hard to get up. However, you do and you both make each day count!<\/p>\n

You are true heroes! I feel honored to know you and to be part of Simon’s Fund.<\/p>\n
