I’m sure that you’ve made so many decisions for your child . . . . what to eat . .\u00a0 . when to go to bed\u00a0. . . whether to play soccer or take piano lessons or both.\u00a0 Hopefully, you’ll never have to make the decision that a friend of friend had to make this week – to pull the plug on their 16 year old son.<\/p>\n

He was playing basketball with some friends.\u00a0 He collapsed in the midst of having fun.\u00a0 He never regained consciousness.\u00a0 With no brain function, his parents made the terrifying decision to stop life support.<\/p>\n

Sudden cardiac arrest kills thousands of children every year.\u00a0 Many of them are engaged in athletic activity.\u00a0 Most of them have no idea that they have a heart condition.<\/p>\n

We can change that.\u00a0 Italy did.\u00a0 They started providing ECG exams for all of their kids.\u00a0 As a result, they reduced the incidents of sudden cardiac arrest by 89%.<\/p>\n

Has your kid received an ECG exam?\u00a0 It costs less than $50.\u00a0 I bet she’s had a hearing exam or eye exam.\u00a0 Has your kid ever fainted?\u00a0 That’s the primary warning sign of a condition that leads to sudden cardiac arrest.<\/p>\n

Please add this to the decisions that you make for your child – get their hearts checked.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
