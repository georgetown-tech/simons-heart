Simon\u2019s Heart is the proud recipient of a $20,000 grant from Phillies Charities, Inc. Grants were provided in part from the support of Phillies fans who participated in the team\u2019s auctions, raffles and memorabilia events held this past baseball season. Upon learning of the grant, Simon\u2019s Heart Executive Director Jenn Parrado said, \u201cThe support of the Philadelphia Phillies means the world to Simon\u2019s Heart because sudden cardiac arrest is the #1 cause of death in youth athletes. We are grateful to share our mission and save lives!\u201d<\/p>\n

The generous grant will be used to provide free heart screenings, AEDs where children learn and play, and CPR Education in communities so that young hearts are protected long into the future. \u201cThese remarkable organizations continuously step up to the plate to be there for those who need it most,\u201d said Phillies Charities, Inc. President John Weber. \u201cThanks to our fans, we are able to help ensure that the much-needed services of our community heroes can continue to make a difference all year long.\u201d<\/p>\n

About Simon\u2019s Heart:
\nSIMON\u2019S HEART is a 501(c)(3) founded in memory of 3-month-old Simon Sudman who died suddenly from an undetected heart condition. The organization is committed to a future in which parents and communities don\u2019t lose children to detectable and treatable heart conditions.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
