Last night, an organization of twenty-five women, threw a party for Simon’s Fund. \u00a0They’ve been having fundraisers for nonprofit organizations for 25 years. Over 200 people gathered to bid on great auction items, eat and drink and watch a fashion show presented by Neiman Marcus. \u00a0We are so grateful. \u00a0Here is the speech that Phyllis shared.<\/em><\/p>\n

When your child dies, you feel alone.\u00a0 No one can comprehend the grief.\u00a0 Few know what to say. \u00a0And then one day, you start to encounter random acts of kindness from strangers that would never have come your way.<\/p>\n

The sixty-something man that came to our house to lead a shiva minyan \u2013 his first time, ever. \u00a0The woman who created our ad book for the very first Simon\u2019s Soiree.\u00a0The student with a heart condition that reached out to volunteer after reading about Simon\u2019s Fund in the paper.\u00a0The company that donated medical equipment from a cold call.<\/p>\n

Then, there\u2019s the story about a bunch of women who just want to help. \u00a0For 25 years, From the Heart has been raising money for local nonprofits.\u00a0 They\u2019ve channeled their time, energy and money toward helping women in need, people living with disease or disability, and children.\u00a0 Their network has raised over $1,000,000.<\/p>\n

We are so humbled and honored to be this year\u2019s beneficiary.\u00a0 Thankfully, most of you in this room will never know what it feels like to lose a child.\u00a0 However, we hope that everyone in this room will one day feel what it is like to be honored by a group of woman with big hearts.\u00a0 It fills your heart.\u00a0 It soothes your soul.<\/p>\n

Thank you so much for honoring our son, Simon, tonight. \u00a0He would have been seven years old and finishing first grade.\u00a0 Thanks for recognizing the efforts and accomplishments of Simon\u2019s Fund.\u00a0 Thanks for helping us raise awareness.\u00a0 Thanks for helping us raise money so that we can continue to check hearts and save lives.<\/p>\n

We\u2019ve prepared a brief video to share with you tonight, but want to take a moment to introduce you to three special groups of people.<\/p>\n

First, will all of the people that have attended a Simon\u2019s Fund event in the past please stand up.\u00a0 These are the people that have funded the heart screenings that have checked almost 4,000 children.\u00a0 Thanks from the bottom of our heart.<\/p>\n

Second, will the Simon\u2019s Fund board members and Jill Deeves please stand up.\u00a0 This dedicated group has helped position Simon\u2019s Fund so that it can impact the lives of children through heart screenings, cookie drives, medical partnership and advocacy efforts.<\/p>\n
