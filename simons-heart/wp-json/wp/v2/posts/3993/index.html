Every screening has a different origin, a different path and a different outcome. \u00a0Our screening at the Shane Victorino Boys and Girls Club was no different. A student that had gotten her heart screened at one of our first heart screenings got a summer internship with the Victorino Foundation. She suggested that we provide a heart screening for the members of the Club. We rarely get the opportunity to do screenings in Philadelphia, so we jumped at the chance.<\/p>\n

We were able to screen 41 students. Two required follow up for suspected heart conditions. We also donated an AED device to their facility.
