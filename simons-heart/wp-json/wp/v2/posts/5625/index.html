Our synagogue honored our work this past weekend by giving us the Keeper of the Flame Award. It’s kind of backwards, because without the support from our friends and community, we would not be in a position to be honored. Phyllis delivered our remarks.<\/p>\n

DARREN AND I ARE VERY HUMBLED AND GRATEFUL FOR THIS AWARD.\u00a0 WE HAVE SO MUCH RESPECT FOR THIS COMMUNITY.\u00a0\u00a0 IT MEANS A LOT TO BE RECOGNIZED BY ALL OF YOU.<\/p>\n

WE JOINED THIS CONGREGATION IN 2002.\u00a0 IT WAS ONE OF THE FIRST THINGS WE DID WHEN WE MOVED TO PHILADELPHIA.<\/p>\n

BEING PART OF A JEWISH COMMUNITY WAS REALLY IMPORTANT TO US.\u00a0 WE ALSO KNEW WE\u2019D NEED THE STANDARD JEWISH FARE OF A BRIS, A BABY NAMING, BNAI MITZVOT AND HEBREW SCHOOL.\u00a0 I GUESS YOU COULD SAY THAT WE CHOSE OR AMI AS OUR SERVICE PROVIDER.<\/p>\n

WE NEVER IMAGINED THAT WE\u2019D NEED OR AMI TO OFFICIATE OUR SON\u2019S FUNERAL, LEAD A SHIVA MINYAN IN OUR HOUSE FOR SEVEN DAYS, UNLEASH A MITZVAH CORPS THAT PROVIDED US WITH FOOD AND NECESSITIES FOR AN ENTIRE MONTH, AND GIVE US A PLACE TO\u00a0 SAY KADDISH FOR A YEAR.<\/p>\n

WE HAD FRIENDS THAT SUPPORTED US, MANY OF YOU ARE HERE TONIGHT, BUT SO MUCH OF THE SUPPORT CAME FROM MEMBERS OF THIS CONGREGATION — THAT WE DID NOT KNOW.\u00a0 WHY?\u00a0 BECAUSE WE WERE JEWISH, LIVING IN THIS COMMUNITY, AND WE WERE IN NEED.<\/p>\n

THINK ABOUT THAT.\u00a0 WHERE ELSE DOES THAT HAPPEN?<\/p>\n

THAT ESSENCE IS REALLY WHAT NEEDS TO BE HONORED HERE TONIGHT.\u00a0 SURE, WE\u2019VE ACCOMPLISHED SOME GOOD THINGS WITH SIMON\u2019S FUND, LIKE HELPING THREE STUDENTS IN THIS CONGREGATION DISCOVER HEART CONDITIONS, BUT WE REALLY DON\u2019T KNOW IF WE\u2019D BE HERE IN THIS POSITION IF IT WEREN\u2019T FOR THE SUPPORT AND COMMITMENT OF THIS COMMUNITY.<\/p>\n

WE WERE WRONG TWELVE YEARS AGO.\u00a0 OR AMI ISN\u2019T JUST A SERVICE PROVIDER.\u00a0 IT\u2019S ALSO AN INSURANCE POLICY.\u00a0 IT\u2019S A PLACE WHERE YOU WILL NEVER BE ALONE IN TIMES OF TRAGEDY OR TRIUPH.<\/p>\n

SO THANKS AGAIN FOR HONORING US TONIGHT.\u00a0 WE ARE PROUD OF THIS AWARD, BUT WE ARE MORE PROUD ABOUT BEING A PART OF THIS JEWISH COMMUNITY.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
