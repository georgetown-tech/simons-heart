<\/a><\/p>\n

Ron Jaworski, former Philadelphia Eagles quarterback and co-host of the 2017 Draft, called out Simon’s Fund<\/a>,\u00a0the Philadelphia School District and Urban Roots for their work in the community. All of us will receive a Legacy Project grant.<\/p>\n

To celebrate this accomplishment, we are giving away two tickets to the upcoming draft for Saturday, April 29! \u00a0<\/strong><\/h4>\n

You’ll get exclusive access to the Eagles Nest – a reserved viewing area at the NFL Draft Experience. To qualify for this drawing, simply\u00a0start a campaign at GotAED<\/a> and get at least one contribution by 4 PM on Friday, April 28. We’ll pick the winner and make arrangements to get you the tickets.<\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

Whether it is heart screenings, advocating for legislation to protect student athletes, gathering data for research, or launching a crowdfunding site\u00a0to distribute AEDs to youth facilities, awareness is at our core. <\/span><\/span><\/p>\n

It\u00a0is incredible that the NFL is willing to lend\u00a0its voice to our cause, and further validate our campaign\u00a0– <\/span><\/span>“You’ve thought about his head. What about his heart?”<\/a><\/span><\/span><\/u><\/p>\n
