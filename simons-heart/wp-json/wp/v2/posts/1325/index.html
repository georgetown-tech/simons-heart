This was the name of the\u00a0 closing\u00a0 session at the Parent Heart Watch Conference.\u00a0 All of the other sessions dealt with AEDs, ECGs, genetic testing, sudden cardiac arrest legislation, etc.\u00a0 They were concrete, factual and steeped in medicine.\u00a0 This session asked us to consider something a little “out there” – has our child been sending us signs?<\/p>\n

Mitch Carmody<\/a>\u00a0lost his son years ago and now travels all over the country speaking to groups of parents that have experienced loss.\u00a0 He challenged our belief system.\u00a0 He asked us to consider\u00a0things like signs, \u00a0orbs and ghosts.\u00a0 He presented us with pictures and stories from parents that had been contacted or visited by their children.<\/p>\n

It’s easy to write off guys like Mitch.\u00a0\u00a0His appearance and message\u00a0is very different than the other presenters:\u00a0 MDs, PhDs and\u00a0MBAs.\u00a0 As I sat in that room, I stood my ground.\u00a0 I could not explain his pictures or explanations.\u00a0 Since, I have reflected over the past six years, and I struggle to explain the following.\u00a0 Maybe you can.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

(1) On a nice September day, Phyllis, Sally and I visited the gravesite.\u00a0 It’s a memorial park, so Sally feels free to run around and play.\u00a0 Here, she is lying on our family stone.\u00a0 Simon is buried underneath.\u00a0 There is an orb of light surrounding Sally’s body.<\/p>\n

(2)\u00a0 On January 24, 2008, Melissa Fair received open heart surgery to fix a heart condition that was detected at a Simon’s Fund heart screening.\u00a0\u00a0January 24th is the day that Simon died.<\/p>\n

(3)\u00a0\u00a0Yesterday, we sat down with a boy named Simon.\u00a0 He is getting ready for his bar mitzvah and wants to do a heart screening at his\u00a0school.<\/p>\n

(4) On October 21, 2012, Drew Harrington had an ablation to correct a condition he discovered at our screening. This is Simon’s birthday.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
