Zachary Syska <\/strong>
\nFort Myers , Florida<\/p>\n

Date of Event: Dec 23, 2017<\/strong><\/p>\n

I was running with my team and I just finished a workout. I felt weird and dizzy, so I sat down near a tree and passed out. I woke up in an ambulance and they told me I went into cardiac arrest. The paramedics told me my coach gave me CPR and EMS shocked me.They took me to Golisano\u2019s Children Hospital. A couple months later I had a procedure for an ICD placement and now I am back to my normal routine. This was a true Christmas Miracle!<\/p>\n

Diagnosis: None.<\/p>\n
