Annie FitzPatrick
\n<\/strong>Philadephia, PA<\/p>\n

Date of Event: February 12, 2016<\/strong><\/p>\n

I was nineteen years old and I stopped by a local convenient store for some coffee. I had just left a doctors appointment. I collapsed and went into cardiac arrest. \u00a0Two police officers were at the store too and had an AED device in their car. \u00a0I had been having seizures for years but the doctors always told me that it was neurological. \u00a0They were wrong. It was a warning sign of my underlying heart condition.<\/p>\n

Diagnosis: \u00a0Long QT Syndrome<\/p>\n
