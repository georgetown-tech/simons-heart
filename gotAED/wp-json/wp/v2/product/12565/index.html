I was invited to be the school nurse at Pace Academy, this school has only been up and running for 1 year. I was to be retired, however I could not decline since the need was urgent. The school nurse that was present was only there for the year and has resigned.
\nMy son attends this specialized charter school for Dyslexia, ADHD, and autism. This school was founded by a mother who was frustrated with the public school system. How could I decline? She is now responsible for running an entire school. I just knew I had to step in and help. I have experienced first hand the cardiac arrest of a school teacher after a school trip. It was traumatizing not having equipment on hand to help.
