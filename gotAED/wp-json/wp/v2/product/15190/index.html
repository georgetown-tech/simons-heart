We serve as a temporary home for survivors of domestic and sexual violence that are experiencing homelessness due to dangerous or violent situations. We provide safe shelter, housing programs, empowerment philosophies, services for children, parent education, and resources for independence. Our goal is to assist in each individuals journey to independence and ending the cycle of domestic and sexual violence and homelessness.<\/p>\n
