St. Cloud Youth Football and Cheer is a non-profit youth sports organization that serves over 500 children year-round. We operate Flag Football and Cheer in the Spring and then Flag Football, Tackle Football, and Cheer in the Fall. We strive to provide a safe and fun environment for our community youth to come out and learn not only a sport but life skills as well. <\/p>\n

This year it is our goal to ensure that we are able to properly respond to first aid and cardiac emergencies anywhere on our property or at our facilities. Combined with proper training, supplies, and an AED we can make this happen.<\/p>\n

Sudden cardiac arrest (SCA) is the leading cause of death in the United States, taking the lives of over 450,000 adults every year. This is more than lung cancer, breast cancer and AIDS, combined. But, SCA is not just an adult thing. It is the leading cause of death of student athletes and takes the lives of thousands of students annually.<\/p>\n
