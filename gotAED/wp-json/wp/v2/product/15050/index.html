Dear Friends and Supporters of Cape Aquatics Swim Club,<\/p>\n

We are reaching out to you today in hopes of raising funds for an important addition to our club: an AED (Automatic External Defibrillator).<\/p>\n

At Cape Aquatics Swim Club, we are dedicated to providing our swimmers of all ages and skill levels with a safe and supportive environment for training and development. In order to continue this mission, we need to ensure the safety of our swimmers, coaches, and spectators in the event of a sudden cardiac arrest.<\/p>\n

An AED is a life-saving device that can detect an irregular heartbeat and administer an electric shock to restore normal heart rhythm. With its ease of use and ability to save lives, an AED is an essential tool for any organization that cares about the safety of its members.<\/p>\n

As a non-profit organization, Cape Aquatics Swim Club relies on the contributions of our parents and generous donations to maintain our facilities and programs. We are committed to making the investment in an AED for our club, but we need your help.<\/p>\n

Please consider making a donation to support the purchase of an AED for Cape Aquatics Swim Club. Your contribution, no matter the amount, will play a critical role in ensuring the safety of our swimmers and community.<\/p>\n

Thank you for your support and consideration.<\/p>\n

Sincerely,<\/p>\n

Neal Saiz
