My name is LeRoy Demarest, aside from my work in the Environmental, Health & Safety Field, I also serve a local congregation in Felton, DE; Grace and Truth Community Church. <\/p>\n

We serve a congregation of approximately 120 individuals, ranging from infants to those in their 80s. Unfortunately, the nearest available AED is approx. five miles down the road at the local fire hall. <\/p>\n

In a cardiac emergency the response time, as good as it is, would be tragically late. We would like to place two highly accessible AEDs in the facility to be prepared for emergencies. This is part of our Emergency Action Plan (EAP)and Cardiac EAP. <\/p>\n

Additionally, I will be training members or our congregation in CPR\/First Aid\/AED to ensure that a number of individuals will be ready to serve the congregation and those in close proximity to our church. <\/p>\n
