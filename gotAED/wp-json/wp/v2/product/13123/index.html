I am the Athletic Director at Volunteer High School in Church Hill, TN. I oversee all athletic events that take place on our campus.<\/p>\n

During the Spring, we have numerous events that can take place at different locations on campus. Our athletic trainer has an AED, but having an additional AED would be beneficial in the event we have a tragedy during a multi-event date.<\/p>\n

For example, last season we had a girls soccer match, tennis match, softball game, and baseball game going at the same time. <\/p>\n

The girls soccer field is at least a 3-5 min golf cart ride up to the baseball field. Having an AED at the top and bottom locations of our campus would save response time in the event something happens. <\/p>\n
