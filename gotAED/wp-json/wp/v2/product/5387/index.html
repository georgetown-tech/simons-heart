My name is Matthew Moskow and for my Bar Mitzvah project I will be raising money to donate an AED to the Laurel House. Growing up, I often hear my Mom on the phone talking to the founder of Simon’s Heart. Additionally, for my bar Mitzvah project, I thought it would be a good idea for me to get involved and donate an AED to the Laurel House. The Laurel House often has many families living there and I thought it would great for them to receive an AED in case of sudden cardiac arrest. The Laurel House needed two AED’s and the first was given by the Philadelphia Eagles and I decided it would be my job to get the second for my Bar Mitzvah project. Thanks so much in advance for your support!<\/p>\n
